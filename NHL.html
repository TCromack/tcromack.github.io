  • NHL Stats Analysis

    A look at NHL player tendencies

    About this project

    View Project

    This project I set out to answer three questions using NHL player statistics.

    Are younger players penalized more than older players? Surprisingly I found that on average younger players take less penalty minutes on average than older players. I think most people have the belief that on average younger players are less disciplined than older players due to lack of experience but when we take a more in depth look at the statistics we can see that is not the case.

    Next I wanted to see what position scores the most Goals on average? The Right Wing players scored the most goals with nearly 32% of all goals scored in the NHL while the Defensemen had a measly 11.6% of all goals scored.

    My favorite team the Boston Bruins are located in the Atlantic Division. I then wanted to know which team in the Atlantic Division team had the youngest team? The Buffalo Sabres had the youngest team at an average age of 25.75 while my Boston Bruins had an average age of 27.85 leaving them as the second oldest team in the Division.

    I was able to come to these conclusions using Excel for the data and then using R functions on that data such as the aggregate function.

    This gave me an average of Penalty minutes by age. I then sorted those results into the age ranges 18 to 23, 24 to 28, 29 to 33 and 34+. The resulting data was visualized using R plot functions as well as a Tableau project to help give the reader a better understanding of the data results.
